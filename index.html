About Tsuda

Tsuda is, as stated before, a Multipurpose Discord Bot made in Discord JS. The bot was only made because I wanted a bot that had such great commands for people who were unable to use since they lacked the role. For instance, you have a private gaming voice channel, but people keep joining to fill up the user limit of, eg: 6. Now you could either contact a mod to change the user limit OR you could use Tsuda and change the user limit to, eg: 5 for League of Legends. Now that pesky one extra person will be unable to rejoin!
If you're looking for a multipurpose bot that can do this and a lot of other things, then look no more. You've come to the right place!

PS: This bot was made because of a late night thinking :)

Join Tsuda in his official Discord server: https://discord.gg/RdTjDVm

Also, Tsuda will not be made available on Github. Consider supporting me on my Patreon if you want access to the source code.

Features

Tsuda for a Profile + Level system

You can view a user's profile and see their level + points. Tsuda will also announce a level up with a cool image. You can also give people reputation points.

Awesome Utility Commands

You can find out where an emoji came from and download it yourself so long as Tsuda is on that server as well, you can also download other users avatars if you think their avatar is cool! You can also find out about users and servers with just 2 simple commands!

A Suffix as an addition

Instead of just being filled with prefixes that are the same as other bots, you can use a suffix instead! Eg: 8ball~ <question>

What Tsuda is currently offering

Kick and Ban those annoying people

Tsuda can be used to kick and ban those people that either break your rules or you just dislike them and you want to kick them.

Fancify your messages!

You can make your messages look fancy with Tsuda's two fancy commands, fancify and ascii.

Sex Jokes as Now Playing
